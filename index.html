Courses Offered

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Focuses on programming, algorithms, software engineering, and cutting-edge technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, and Cybersecurity to solve real-world problems and drive digital transformation.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

Emphasizes VLSI design, communication systems, and emerging technologies like IoT, Embedded Systems, and wireless communication to design smarter devices and systems for a connected world.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

Involves power systems, control engineering, and renewable energy technologies, preparing students to innovate and manage modern energy solutions for a sustainable future.

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

Covers mechanical design, robotics, thermal engineering, and manufacturing technologies to drive innovation in industrial and automotive systems.

Civil Engineering

Focuses on structural design, transportation systems, and sustainable infrastructure development using modern tools like AutoCAD, BIM, and environmental analysis.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Equips students with leadership, strategic management, financial analysis, and entrepreneurial skills, preparing them to excel in global business environments.

About Us

Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed institution renowned for academic excellence and discipline. Sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, it runs 18 educational institutions across diverse fields of study.

The college offers B.Tech programs in various disciplines such as Computer Science Engineering (CSE), CSE - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML), Information Technology (IT), and many others. It also offers M.Tech programs and MBA.

Located in the well-connected city of Vijayawada, the campus spans 19.98 acres, with state-of-the-art infrastructure and laboratories. The institution is autonomous, accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade, and follows ISO 9001-2015 standards.
